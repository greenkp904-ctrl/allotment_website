About the College

Established in 1995, Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering is one of Kerala’s leading engineering institutions, known for academic excellence, innovation, and student-centered growth. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in multiple engineering streams, fostering a strong culture of research and technical advancement.

KSRTC Quota

The KSRTC quota provides admission opportunities for children of Kerala State Road Transport Corporation employees, maintaining fair allotment and academic standards.

NRI Quota

The NRI quota offers admission to students sponsored by Non-Resident Indians, maintaining strong global Malayali community ties and access to high-quality technical education.
